# AntiGravity SCM - Implementation Plan

## Project Title
AntiGravity SCM - Smart Supply Chain Management System

---

# 1. Introduction

AntiGravity SCM is a smart supply chain management system designed to manage products, suppliers, warehouses, inventory, orders, shipments, and analytics in a centralized platform.

The system automates stock management, monitors deliveries, generates inventory alerts, and predicts future demand using historical sales data.

---

# 2. Objectives

- Manage products, suppliers, warehouses, and orders
- Track inventory in real time
- Automate stock updates
- Generate low-stock alerts
- Monitor shipments and deliveries
- Analyze sales and inventory movement
- Predict future product demand
- Improve warehouse and supply chain efficiency
